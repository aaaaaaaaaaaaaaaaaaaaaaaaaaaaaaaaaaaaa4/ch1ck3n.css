ch1ck3n.css

The CSS stylesheet for looking good

How to use

Put 
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/ch1ck3n-byte/ch1ck3n.css@main/ch1ck3n.css">
into your head tag.

Container

A container contains text so it breaks on to the next line after a certain amount of width.

I'm outside a container

I'm inside the conatiner

You can also use different container sizes such as 
container-20, container-40, container-50
.

Loaders

Loaders in ch1ck3n.css are spinning elements. It can be applied to anything. 
<img class="spin-loader" src="shrek.ico">

Circle spinners

<span class="spinner"></span>

Buttons

Buttons in ch1ck3n.css are simple but very good looking buttons.


<button class="btn" onClick="alert('you clicked me')">Click me</button>

Links

Links are pretty simple.
Click me!
<a href="https://ch1ck3n.com">Click me!</a>
